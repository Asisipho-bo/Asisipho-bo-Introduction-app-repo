About me!

I am Asisipho Boniwe Msitho, I am currently part of the Zaio.io practical job training program. I am originally from eMqanduli in the Eastern Cape and I stay in Khayelitsha, I have Bachelor's degree in Psychology from the University of Cape Town. I graduated last year and worked as an Education program assistant/researcher for the Stellenbosch Triennale. It was during this time that I explored and looked into other things that interest me. I am a lover of the arts, I write poetry, short stories and plays and I spent some performing while I was still in University. I believe we learn until the day we die and because of this I will never pass up an opportunity to learn, I will always strive to be more, to be better and to stay willing.
